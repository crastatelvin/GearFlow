# GearFlow Master Service Agreement (MSA)

**Last Updated:** May 2026

This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between **GearFlow Autonomous Systems** ("Company") and the **Independent Service Provider** ("Mechanic").

## 1. Scope of Services
The Mechanic agrees to provide bike repair and maintenance services as dispatched through the GearFlow platform.

## 2. Payout Structure
- **Mechanic Share:** 70% of total job value (excluding diagnostic fee).
- **GearFlow Commission:** 30% for platform maintenance, insurance, and lead generation.
- **Payment Schedule:** Payouts are rolled out to the Mechanic's wallet in the first week of every month.

## 3. Code of Conduct & Zero-Trust Policies
- **No Direct Dealing:** Mechanics are strictly prohibited from sharing personal contact information or accepting payments outside the GearFlow platform. Violation results in an immediate and permanent ban.
- **Verification:** All services must be verified via the GearFlow AI Vision system and OTP confirmation from the customer.
- **Device Security:** The GearFlow app must be run on a non-rooted, physical mobile device with an active SIM card.

## 4. Liability
Mechanics are independent contractors. GearFlow provides a platform for dispatch and does not employ mechanics directly.

## 5. Termination
Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days' notice. GearFlow reserves the right to terminate immediately for breach of security or fraud policies.
